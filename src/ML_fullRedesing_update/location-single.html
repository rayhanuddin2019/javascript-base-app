NOSSO Summer Club Marbella

Welcome to Nosso Summer Club Marbella, the ultimate destination for a luxurious beach club experience. Located on the stunning Mediterranean Sea, the club offers a wide range of facilities, including a restaurant, lounge areas, and beach beds. The chic and stylish ambiance creates a relaxed yet refined atmosphere that is perfect for soaking up the sun and indulging in ultimate relaxation.

As a guest, you'll enjoy exclusive amenities and exceptional dining options, all while surrounded by the pristine golden sand beach and natural beauty of the area. Whether you're looking to relax, socialise, or simply soak up the ambiance, the team will welcome you at Nosso Summer Club Marbella and indulge in the ultimate in beach club luxury.

Introduction

Located east of Marbella, you will find Nosso Summer Club Marbella. It's a prime destination for those seeking a luxurious beach club experience. With its stunning location on the Mediterranean Sea, the club offers a wide range of facilities, including a restaurant, lounge areas and beach beds, all set against a breathtaking backdrop. Here's what you need to know about this number one Marbella beach club.

About the Beach Club

The Nosso Summer Club Marbella Beach Club is known for its elegant and sophisticated atmosphere. With a focus on luxury and comfort, the club caters to those looking for an unforgettable beach club experience. The beach club is open everyday and also allows children and dogs. The beach club provides Single beds, cabanas and a chill out lounge.

MarbsLifestyle.com's review: All time favorite Nosso Summer Club

For the connoisseurs at MarbsLifestyle.com, Nosso Summer Club emerges as the undisputed favorite among Marbella's plethora of beach clubs. It isn't merely the sun, sand, and sea that beckons, but the unparalleled vibe that Nosso emanates. Every visit feels like an indulgence, a testament to the club's meticulously curated atmosphere.

The diverse menu, an epitome of culinary craftsmanship, promises a gastronomic journey with every dish. But it's not just the food that's exceptional; Nosso's cocktails are an art form in themselves. With each sip, one is transported to a world of flavors, making every Friday visit almost a sacred ritual for us.

What elevates the Nosso experience further is its impeccable service and ambience. Whether you choose a beach bed to lounge and soak in the sun, a dedicated lounge area to engage in intimate conversations, or a restaurant table to immerse in the culinary delights, Nosso ensures perfection. Friendly staff, ever eager to enhance your experience, coupled with the mellifluous beats from a skilled DJ, craft an ambiance that's both invigorating and soothing. Nosso isn't just a destination; it's an experience, a sentiment, a lifestyle.

Ambiance of the Beach Club

Experience pure luxury and exclusivity at the Nosso Summer Club Marbella Beach Club, where chic and stylish ambiance meets stunning natural surroundings. The modern and tasteful decor, complete with sleek loungers, comfortable cabanas, and a contemporary beach area, create a relaxed yet refined atmosphere that is perfect for soaking up the sun and indulging in ultimate relaxation. Surrounded by the breathtaking beauty of the Mediterranean Sea golden sand beach is the perfect destination for a day of pure bliss. Book your spot today with us and indulge in the ultimate sophisticated and luxurious beach club experience that Nosso Summer Club Marbella has to offer.

Restaurant

The Nosso Summer Club Marbella Beach Club's restaurant is a standout feature of the club, offering an exceptional dining experience in a breathtaking setting. With a focus on fresh and locally sourced ingredients, the menu features a range of Mediterranean and international dishes, all expertly prepared by the club's talented chefs. The restaurant is open in high season for breakfast, lunch and dinner and in low season only during lunch time. With a selection of wines and cocktails available to complement your meal.

Facilities

The Nosso Summer Club Marbella Beach Club offers a wide range of facilities, ensuring that there's something for everyone. The club features towel service, sun loungers, cabanas, and beach side service. The beach club also has a shop, they sell some club's merchandise , nice heads, beachwear and some other nice items.

Events

Nosso Summer Club Marbella occasionally organises events, for which they sometimes charge a different price. If you want to know more information about this, check the events calendar on the website of marbslifestyle.com.

Location of the Beach Club

The Nosso Summer Club Marbella Beach Club is located on Av. del Limonar, 124, 29604 Marbella, Málaga, Spain. Situated just a few minutes' drive from the centre of Marbella, the club is easily accessible by car or taxi.

Parking

The Nosso Summer Club Marbella Beach Club offers no parking to their guests, but it is easy to find a parking spot in the streets of El Rosario.

Entry

Nosso Summer Club never actually asks for an entry fee, but this can be the case when there is a special event. The dress code at Nosso Summer club is Summer chic.

Conclusion

The Nosso Summer Club Marbella Beach Club is a luxurious and sophisticated destination for those seeking a premium beach club experience. From the stunning surroundings and chic ambiance to the exceptional restaurant and range of facilities, the club offers everything you need for a day of relaxation and indulgence. As a guest, you're sure to enjoy the club's exclusive atmosphere and high-end amenities. When planning your Marbella getaway, don't forget to include the Nosso Summer Club Marbella on your must-visit list and immerse yourself one of the best beach club experience of Marbella.
